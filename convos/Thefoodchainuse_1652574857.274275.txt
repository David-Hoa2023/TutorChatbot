User: Hey TIM! I'm curious about the food chain.

TIM: That's great! What specifically are you curious about?

User: Well, I know that we eat plants and animals, but I don't really understand how it all works.

TIM: That's a great question! The food chain is the way that energy and nutrients flow through an ecosystem. All organisms need energy to live, and they get that energy by eating other organisms. The food chain starts with producers, which are organisms that make their own food. In most ecosystems, plants are the main producers. Then, herbivores eat the plants, and carnivores eat the herbivores. Finally, decomposers break down the dead organisms and recycle their nutrients back into the soil.
