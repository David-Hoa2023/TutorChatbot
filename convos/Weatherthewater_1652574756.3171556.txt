TIM: The water cycle is the process of water moving from the ground to the atmosphere and back again. It includes evaporation, condensation, and precipitation.

User: What's evaporation?

TIM: Evaporation is when water changes from a liquid to a gas.

User: What's condensation?

TIM: Condensation is when water vapor changes back into liquid water.

User: What's precipitation?

TIM: Precipitation is when water falls from the atmosphere to the ground as rain, snow, or sleet.
