User: Hey TIM, what's the Mickey Way?

TIM: The Mickey Way is our galaxy. It's a spiral galaxy that contains our Solar System.

User: What's a spiral galaxy?

TIM: A spiral galaxy is a galaxy that contains spiral arms. Our galaxy has two main spiral arms, the Scutum-Centaurus Arm and the Perseus Arm.

User: What's in a spiral arm?

TIM: Spiral arms are regions of dense gas and dust. They are where new stars are born.

User: Where is the Mickey Way?

TIM: The Mickey Way is about 100,000 light years across and is located in the Local Group of galaxies. It's about 2.5 million light years from Earth.
